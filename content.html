Despite recent advances in AI, there remains a gap between what an AI model is capable of and how to harness such AI capabilities to serve humanity. To close this gap, my research takes a human-centered approach to design, build, and study interactive AI systems that align with human values, assimilate human intents, and augment human abilities.

My work has been supported by NSF CAREER, ONR YIP, Google Research Scholar Award, Intel Rising Star Award, Hellman Fellowship, and NSF CRII Award.
