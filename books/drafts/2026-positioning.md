<!-- What is positioning -->
Overall, positioning is about answering the question: how are you different?
> ... positioning is not what you do to a product. ... you position the product in the mind of the prospect.

The book talks about how the first the enter a market has immense power over subsequent players. however, in the tech world, there have been some counter examples:
- yahoo is the first to enter the search engine market but google came later and took over
- openai was the first to enter the llm chatbot market but others (google gemini, anthropic claude, xai grok) followed suite and pretty much evened the game

the chapter about Jamaica is worth revisiting---a very nice example that shows positioning is as important, if not more so, than the substance
