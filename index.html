The core of my research is computational interaction design:

  1. I design novel ways of interacting with devices or applications, ...
  2. explore enabling technology to implement them, ...
  3. propose computational methods to solve problems along the way, ...
  4. and develop metrics to evaluate the performance.
My research has spanned an ecosystem of devices, from stylus, to wearable and mobile devices, to appliances, and to fabrication machines such as 3D printers.
